About

Welcome to our eMarket! We showcase and sell a wide range of stunning 3D models for various applications, from gaming to architectural visualizations. Our platform connects talented creators with buyers seeking high-quality models, making it easy to find the perfect 3D asset for your projects. Explore our collection and elevate your creative endeavors with us!

This web application was developed by Nick J. Petrone and Luis Cruz Sanchez as part of the Washington Vets to Tech (WaV2T) program. We developed the application over the course of a few weeks utilizing the Django framework, gaining significant insights into web application development in the process. Luis took the lead on the front-end, implementing the 3D-model display, cart functionality, and most of the theming. Meanwhile, Nick focused on the back-end, handling user accounts, authentication, and profile functionalities.
